# README.md

This folder contains the TEMPEST and synoptic site water potential data,
as submitted to PSInet (https://psinetrcn.github.io).


Water potential data were collected from COMPASS synoptic site: Moneystump Swamp (MSM) from roughly May to November during 2022 and 2023. Samples were collected from Loblolly pines (PITA) in the transition and upland zones.
